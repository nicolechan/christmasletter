Iceland

Our big family trip this year was to Iceland. It was a complete outdoor adventure. We drove the relatively narrow two-lane roads across a large portion of the main island. The diversity of geography was remarkable. With 20 hours of daylight, we had spectacular views of mountains, fjords, waterfalls, volcanoes and geysers. The highlight for the girls (definitely NOT Patty) was rafting the whitewater of the frigid East Glacial River. The dry suits definitely kept us warm and dry but didn't prevent Patty from falling out of the raft a couple times and taking a fast swim downstream before being picked up by the rescue kayaker. Patty was fine. Needless to say, Patty's rafting days are over. In this photo, that's Tom and Alex in the front. Patty is behind Alex...still in the raft!
